BSBCMM511 AND INFLUENCE COMMUNICATION

Communication engineering is developed through the mutual penetration and complementarity of specialties such as wired telecommunications, wireless communication, electronic technology, and postal and telecommunications communication. The knowledge system of the Communication Engineering major includes general knowledge, professional subject knowledge, , practical teaching, etc. The curriculum should support the achievement of training objectives, and the curriculum system should support the effective achievement of various graduation requirements.

BSBLDR523 LEADS AND MANAGES EFFECTIVE WORKPLACE RELATIONSHIPS

The work of management is to plan and budget, organize and allocate personnel, control and solve problems, with the aim of establishing order. The job of leadership is to determine direction, integrate stakeholders, motivate and inspire employees, with the aim of generating change. The curriculum is designed to cultivate students' leadership and management abilities.

BSBTWK502 MANAGEMENT TEAM EFFICIENCY

This course fundamentally breaks down personal communication barriers, helps individuals master effective communication skills, understand the differences between groups and teams, and master the ways and methods to build high-performance teams Clarify the characteristics of high-performance teams, master the methods of building a learning oriented team, build a positive and healthy team culture, master the role models and behavior shaping methods of team members, enhance team morale, improve team effectiveness through mastering communication and coordination skills, understand the main reasons for team conflicts, master the ideas and strategies for handling conflicts, build a team vision, implement team strategies, and manage high-performance teams.

BSBCRT511 CULTIVATES OTHERS' CRITICAL THINKING

This course is conducive to improving the level of intellectual education, focusing on cultivating cognitive abilities, promoting thinking development, and stimulating innovation awareness. Encourage students to apply their knowledge, abilities, and literacy to solve practical problems. Examining students' ability to make novel speculations and assumptions, as well as to conduct thorough argumentation; Examining students' ability to explore new methods and actively solve problems, encouraging them to break free from the constraints of their thinking and innovate boldly.

BSBOPS502 MANAGES BUSINESS OPERATIONS PLANS

This course is beneficial for students to have an early understanding of society, effectively carry out business operations, and enable enterprises to have a competitive advantage over other competitors in the market. It can help students better understand the employment environment in society, find the best practices to achieve results and optimize organizational performance.
